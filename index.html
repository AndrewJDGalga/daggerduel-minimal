Dagger Duel - Digital Prototype Version 1

Rules

Goal

Defeat your opponent:

  • Reduce your opponent's health to 0.
  • Capture enough pieces that your opponent is unable to play any tokens on their turn.

How to play

First turn (Black)

  • The Black player must place 2 pieced to start the game.
    • Both pieces must match Black's face up Footing Tracker.

    • The first piece must go into the marked center space:
    • The second piece may go anywhere on the board.
  • Once Black has placed 2 pieces, play proceeds to White.

Following turns

  1. Flip your Footing Tracker to the opposite side.
  2. Select 4 tokens matching your Footing Tracker's current facing from your Token Supply. These represent Potential Actions.
  3. Review your abilities and decide on your moves for the turn:
    • You may place all or some of your 4 Potential Action tokens face up onto any unoccupied hexes on the board. This includes Barrier hexes. Barrier hexes cannot be surrounded by pieces, and so pieces on them are completely safe from Normal Capture:
    • You may return 1 of your 4 Potential Action tokens to your Token Supply to replace 1 of your Opponent's tokens that doesn't match their current Footing with 1 of your other Potential Action tokens.
    • You may return 1 of your 4 Potential Action tokens to your Token Supply and flip 1 other of your Potential Action tokens to the Faceless side to use your Technique action.
      • Technique icon:
      • Faceless Token Example:
      • Faceless Tokens are exactly like Normal Tokens except they possess no Facing and cannot be protected by your current Footing.
    • If your Special Meter is full, you may: Spend all of your Special Meter, return 1 of your 4 Potential Action tokens to your Token Supply, and flip 1 other of your Potential Action tokens to the Faceless side to use your Special action.
      • Your Special Meter begins the game empty and is full at 2.
      • Special Meter icon:
    • If your Anger Meter is full, you may: Spend all of your Anger Meter, return 1 of your 4 Potential Action tokens to your Token Supply, and flip 1 other of your Potential Action tokens to the Faceless side to use your Special action.
      • Your Anger Meter begins the game empty and is full at 2.

Capturing and Damage

Normal Capture

The only way you can deal damage to your opponent is through Normal Capture of their pieces. Normal Capture occurs whenever you successfully surround your Opponent's pieces and:

  • Your pieces, regardless of any empty space, completely encircle your Opponent's pieces.
  • The pieces you've surrounded don't match your Opponent's Footing indicator.
    • In order to Normal Capture Tokens that currently match your Opponent's Footing indicator, you must wait until the Start of your Next Turn or use an Ability. If, on the Start of your turn, there are any Tokens belonging to your Opponent that are encircled and don't match your Opponent's Footing, you Normal Capture them.

Valid Normal Captures are resolved immediately during your turn, and each successful Normal Capture deals 1 damage to your Opponent's Health regardless of the number of pieces captured.

After resolving a Capture you initiated, increase your Special Meter by 1. Your Opponent increases their Anger meter by 1.

Special Capture

Special Capture occurs whenever you remove Opponent Tokens from the board through Abilities instead of surrounding them. Keep the pieces gained through Special Captures, and you gain Special Meter and your Opponent Gains Anger Meter as normal. However, no damage is done to your Opponent's Health.
